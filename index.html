ABOUT US

Hold 2 Holds created out of a simple love for climbing and the people around it. What began as shared sessions on the wall and endless conversations about movement, holds, and training slowly grew into an idea: to build a place made by climbers, for climbers.

We focus on climbing holds and equipment that we genuinely trust and use ourselves - solid, well-designed, and built to last. Every product in our shop is tested in real conditions and shaped by our own experience on the wall.

No matter if you’re just starting out or working on your next project, we’re here to support your climbing journey and grow together with the community.

OUR TEAM

Nina is the creative heart of Hold 2 Holds. She pays attention to the details - how a hold feels under your fingers, how movement flows between shapes, and how climbing can be both challenging and fun. For her, climbing is as much about creativity as it is about strength.

When she’s not working on new ideas for the shop, she’s usually climbing, sketching concepts, or finding inspiration outdoors.

Kinga is the analytical and strategic mind of the team. She’s passionate about training smart, understanding movement, and building equipment that truly helps climbers progress. Her approach is practical, focused, and always rooted in real climbing needs.

Outside of work, Kinga enjoys pushing her limits on the wall, testing new training methods, and sharing knowledge with others.

Mateusz is the technical backbone of Hold 2 Holds. He loves problem-solving, testing ideas, and making sure everything works exactly as it should - from the durability of holds to the small details that often go unnoticed. His climbing style reflects his mindset: precise, patient, and thoughtful.

When Mateusz isn’t working on improvements and new solutions, you’ll find him climbing, experimenting with setups, or fine-tuning the gear behind the scenes.
